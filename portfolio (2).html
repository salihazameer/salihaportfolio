Who I am

I sit where strategy, story, and technology meet — and I ship the whole thing myself.

For years I've run creative and content for a global beauty brand operating across 75+ markets: directing campaigns, building the SEO and content engine, and managing the storefronts that turn attention into revenue. No hand-offs between teams — the idea, the film, the page, and the numbers all live in one place.

What sets my work apart now is AI. I use generative tools to produce broadcast-grade campaign films in days instead of quarters, then wrap them in content systems and conversion thinking so they actually perform. I care about taste as much as throughput.
